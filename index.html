Our Services

👩‍⚕️

Personal Care

Support with Eating, Toileting, Walking, Transferring, Positioning, Personal Hygiene, Dressing, and Bathing. We encourage independence while providing assistance as needed.

💊

Medication Services

Medication reminders, assistance with oral medications, and total support with administration when required. Staff are trained and delegated in various tasks.

🏥

Skilled Nursing & Delegation

Access to skilled nursing through licensed RNs and delegators. Staff may perform delegable nursing tasks under state guidelines.

🧠

Specialty Care

Specialized care for residents with developmental disabilities, mental illness, and dementia, with attention to individual needs.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Staffing & Support

24-hour staffing based on resident needs. Awake night staff and additional caregiver support available. All staff complete required Washington State training.

🌍

Cultural & Language Access

English is the primary language. Staff respect and accommodate residents’ cultural beliefs, practices, and meal preferences when possible.

💵

Medicaid Policy

We accept Medicaid after 12 months of private pay with advanced notice. Some beds are available for private pay residents converting to Medicaid.

🎨

Activities

Engaging activities tailored to resident preferences and interests, fostering purpose, motivation, and well-being.

“Where love and laughter live.”
